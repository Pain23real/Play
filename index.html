How to Play The Secret Jump

In The Secret Jump, you control a character who must jump on umbrellas to climb as high as possible. The higher you climb, the more points you earn!

1

Start the Game

Enter your nickname and press the "Start Game" button to begin the adventure

2

Jump on Umbrellas

Use arrow keys (or touch buttons) to move left and right

3

Difficulty Levels

The higher you climb, the more difficult the game becomes and the higher your level

4

Records Table

Your result is recorded in the leaderboard where you can see your rank among other players
